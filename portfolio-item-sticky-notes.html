SuperSticky Notes

React App

Live Site

ES6 | JSX | Managing Data Flow | Manipulating the DOM

How many sticky notes do you have fluttering around? This app helps keep all your thoughts in one place.

Take as many notes as you want. So many reminders that you forgot when to pick mom up at the airport? No worries, your data is stored to local storage. Just use the search function to find just the note you need. You can add, delete, and save to help preserve your memory and sanity.
